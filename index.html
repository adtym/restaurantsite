welcome to curcuma

Delivering authentic indian food for more than 50 years!

Fame of our quality food soon spread far and wide and our market presence expanded with opening of new outlets one after the other.

It all started way back in the mid twentieth century, when a small part of our family ventured out of Kerala and came to the city of Delhi. They brought along with them an ancestral wealth recipes of traditional Indian foods developed over generations by our forefathers.
